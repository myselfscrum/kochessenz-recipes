My wife wants dessert! So I conjure from these leftovers

* 1 orange, filleted 
* 1 tablespoon yogurt 
* 1 piece of vanilla ice cream 
* 1 tsp sea buckthorn juice 
* some flaxseed 
* some hazelnut, ground 
* 1 small sprig of mint

a small, quick dessert.
